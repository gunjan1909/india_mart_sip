About Jhas Industries

Since our inception in the year 1949 as Jhas Industries, an ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 14001:2015 certified sole proprietorship organisation, we have gained more than sixty years of vast experience with an explicitly defined objective of Manufacturing, Exporting and Supplying a large assortment of Security Seals, Plastic Seals, Polycarbonate Seals, Meter Seals, Wire Seals, Lead Seals and more.

Our products are extensively used for security applications and are highly appreciated for their tamper-proof attributes. Our vast range of quality seals finds applications in energy distribution, forestry, judiciary, meter manufacturing, inspection, and many more industries. We serve clients across India and globally.
