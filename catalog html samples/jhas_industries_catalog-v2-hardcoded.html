About Us

JHAS Industries is India's leading security seal manufacturer with over 60+ years of experience, delivering reliable sealing solutions to clients across the globe. Founded in 1949 in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, JHAS has grown from a local manufacturer to an internationally recognized exporter of high-quality security seals.

Our product portfolio spans 15+ categories including Bag Seals, Bolt Seals, Cable Seals, Container Seals, Meter Seals, Plastic Seals, Wire Seals, and more — offering comprehensive tamper-evident and security solutions for logistics, utilities, banking, retail, and industrial sectors.

We combine decades of craftsmanship with modern manufacturing processes to ensure every seal meets the highest standards of security and reliability.
